Why choose us?

O2Drops prides itself on the ability to provide clean and safe to drink water to all our clients.
Our water undergoes a 6-stage purification process that removes
harmful contaminents like heavy metals.

Sediment Filteration

Removes dirt, sludge and
other undissolved solids.

Element Filteration

A 5-micron filter element removes all undissolved solids;
and a second stage 1-micron element filter the further removes
all the remaining undissolved solids.

Reverse Osmosis

Removes 98% of all undissolved solids,
including sodium.

Carbon Filteration

Removes pesticides, chlorine and
other organic chemicals.

UV Protection

The UV light sterilises the water
before ozonation takes place.

Ozonation

Ozone is injected into the water, killing all the bacteria and
viruses while sterilising the container. Since when ozone mixes with water,
it turns into oxygen, our water has increased oxygen levels.
